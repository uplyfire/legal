# Data Deletion — Uplyfire Apps

This page applies to: **Image to WebP**, **HEIC Converter**, **Video Compressor**, and **Photo to PDF**.

All file processing occurs entirely on your device. We do not upload, access, or store your files on any server.

## How to delete your data

1. **Clear App cache and data**: Go to Android Settings > Apps > [App name] > Storage > Clear Data.
2. **Uninstall the App**: Removing the App deletes all locally stored data, including preferences and cache.

## Data managed by third parties

- **Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics**: Analytics data is retained by Google for up to 14 months and crash data for 90 days. To manage your data, visit [Google Account Settings](https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy).
- **Google AdMob**: To manage or delete advertising data, visit [Google Account Settings](https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy).
- **Google Play**: Purchase history is managed by Google Play. Visit [Google Play Order History](https://play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory).
