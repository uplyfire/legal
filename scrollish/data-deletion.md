# Data Deletion — Scrollish

Scrollish stores your learning data — your progress, streaks, saved cards, achievements, settings, and the optional name you choose — **locally on your device**. We do not keep this data on our servers and we do not maintain user accounts.

The only information that leaves your device is (a) the schedule for the optional reminder notifications you turn on, and (b) — only if you consent — anonymous analytics and crash diagnostics. This page explains how to delete all of it.

## How to delete your on-device data

1. **Delete all data in-app:** Open **Settings → "Delete all my data"** and confirm in the dialog. This permanently deletes all your progress, streaks, settings, and your optional name from the device.
2. **Clear all app data:** Go to Android Settings → Apps → Scrollish → Storage → Clear Data.
3. **Uninstall the App:** Removing the App deletes all locally stored data from your device.

## How to delete your reminder-scheduling record

If you enabled daily reminders, a minimal record (a push token, your time zone, and your chosen reminder times, keyed to an anonymous identifier — **no name, no learning data, no email**) is stored on our reminder backend.

- **Turn reminders off:** In **Settings → Notifications**, disable reminders. This instructs our backend to delete the record for your device.
- **Uninstall the App:** Uninstalling stops reminders but does **not** by itself delete the backend record. To delete it, turn reminders off (Settings → Notifications) **before** uninstalling, or email us (below) and we will delete it.

## Data managed by third parties

- **Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics (Google):** Collected only with your consent. To manage or delete data associated with your device, visit [Google Account — Data & Privacy](https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy). You can also withdraw consent at any time in **Settings → "Privacy & ad settings."**
- **Google Play:** Subscription and purchase history is managed by Google Play. Visit [Google Play Order History](https://play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory).
