## 2. Description of the App

Scrollish is an educational application for learning English through a feed of interactive learning cards. Depending on the version and available updates, the App may include some or all of the following features:

- A scrollable feed of interactive learning cards in a variety of formats (such as flashcards, multiple choice, true/false swipes, fill-the-gap, and other card types)
- A spaced-repetition learning system that schedules reviews based on your answers
- Lessons and modules organized into a course, with progression and unlocking
- Progress tracking, daily streaks, daily goals, streak shields, experience points, and achievements
- Saved cards, optional daily reminder notifications, and progress backup and transfer (for subscribers)

Features may be added, modified, or removed over time. The specific features available to you depend on the version of the App you have installed and whether you have an active subscription.

The App is available with a free tier (which provides access to a portion of the content) and a premium tier ("Scrollish Premium") available through an auto-renewing subscription, as described in Section 4.
