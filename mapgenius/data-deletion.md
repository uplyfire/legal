# Data Deletion — MapGenius

MapGenius stores all data locally on your device. We do not maintain server-side user accounts or data.

## How to delete your data

1. **Reset progress in-app**: Open Settings > tap "Reset Progress" > confirm in the dialog. This deletes all game history, scores, and statistics.
2. **Clear all App data**: Go to Android Settings > Apps > MapGenius > Storage > Clear Data.
3. **Uninstall the App**: Removing the App deletes all locally stored data.

## Data managed by third parties

- **Google AdMob**: To manage or delete data collected by Google for advertising, visit [Google Account Settings](https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy).
- **Google Play**: Purchase history is managed by Google Play. Visit [Google Play Order History](https://play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory).
